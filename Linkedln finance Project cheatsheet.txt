What NORM.INV does:

NORM.INV(RAND(), mean, std_dev)

RAND()    → generates random number 0-1
mean      → your expected value (3%)
std_dev   → how much it can vary (1%)

Result: random growth rate that's usually
near 3% but sometimes 2% or 4%
Realistic randomness!
